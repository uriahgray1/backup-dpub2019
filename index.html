OPEN FOR PUBLIC is a network of user-run public websites for public good developed by MADA Graphic Design students. As the traditional functions of public spaces have been replaced by walled off private websites, we must reclaim these common public goods and services. These websites act as public parks not private homes, they provide community services, local knowledge and a genuine social network. These websites reclaim the market, the library, the noticeboard, the directory, the school, the stage, the park, the club, and the forum.
